zPass2 is a password app that helps you generate unique passwords for different websites and applications login.

It works locally in your iPhone and iPad and no one knows about it. It never saves any information.

All your passwords will be generated on the moment using one secret Keyword that you define.

Every time you need to login somewhere you use zPass2 to retrieve your password by typing your Keyword and a website or app name.

The generated passwords are strong passwords that you never need to remember. Just generate them every time you need to use them.

Since there is no saved information anywhere you can use multiple devices to generate your passwords without any setup needed.
